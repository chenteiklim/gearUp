When you interact with our website, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect details such as your name, email address, shipping address.

  • Device Information: We collect data from the devices and browsers you use to access our site, including IP address, device type, and browser type.

We use your information to provide, improve, and personalize our services. Specifically, we may use your data to:

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following scenarios:

4. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access and Correction: You can access, update, or correct your personal information through your account settings.
  • Data Deletion: You can request that we delete your data, subject to certain exceptions for legal and business purposes.

  • Cookies: You can control cookie settings in your browser. Note that disabling cookies may affect the website’s functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures such as encryption, access control, and regular security audits to protect your data. While we strive to secure your personal information, no security method is completely infallible, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to fulfill orders, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. When no longer needed, your data will be deleted or anonymized.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
